ORCHIDS

Orchids are a diverse and highly specialized group of flowering plants known for their stunning and often intricate blooms. They belong to the Orchidaceae family, which is one of the largest families of flowering plants, with thousands of species and even more hybrids and cultivars

Gerbera

Gerbera flowers, commonly known as Gerbera daisies, are popular and vibrant flowering plants that belong to the Asteraceae family. They are native to South America, Africa, and Asia. Gerberas are widely cultivated for their large, colorful blooms and are often used in gardens, floral arrangements, and as cut flowers.

Gypsophila

Gypsophila, commonly known as baby's breath, is a delicate and popular flowering plant often used as a filler in floral arrangements and bouquets. It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is native to Eurasia, but it is now cultivated and appreciated worldwide for its charming appearance.
